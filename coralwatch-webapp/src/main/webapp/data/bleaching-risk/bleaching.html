Surveys with a potential bleaching risk

CoralWatch has conducted statistical analysis comparing 10 years of our data to what is known about well-established bleaching events. This validation analysis has allowed us to develop a new evidence-based approach to classifying our data. Surveys which indicate a potential risk of bleaching are listed below. If a single survey indicates a 'potential bleaching risk', this does not mean that bleaching is definitely occurring. Based on our analyses, each of the surveys listed below has a 70% likelihood of representing a small bleaching event. More than one survey is necessary to confirm a bleaching event. If multiple surveys from the same region indicate potential bleaching risk, then this increases the likelihood that a true bleaching event is occurring.
